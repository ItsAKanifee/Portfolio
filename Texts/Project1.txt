Developed a video game controller using the Muse EEG headband to interpret 
brainwave data and control gameplay. Utilized Python and Bluetooth communication 
to process real-time EEG signals. Designed and tested custom signal processing 
algorithms. Enabled hands-free interaction, demonstrating innovative applications 
of brain-computer interfaces, as well as showcasing ways to innovate video game accessibility for 
those without full use of their hands
